Terry Dietrick

Terry Dietrick was born in Lansing, Michigan. He was raised in a small town named Holt just south of the capital city. He is an avid high performance import sports car enthusiasts and is currently building his own dragracing vehicle. He initially joined the military at the age of 20 in 2004 and is currently exiting the active military service in hopes of becoming a web designer in the future.
